================================================================================
              🚀 WARRIORS ROCKET ADVENTURE
                 KID'S QUICK START GUIDE
================================================================================

Hey there, future rocket scientist! 👋

Ready to become a certified rocket pilot? Let's get started!

================================================================================
                         STEP 1: LAUNCH THE APP
================================================================================

Find the file called "LAUNCH_ACADEMY.bat" and DOUBLE-CLICK it!

OR

Open "RocketryAcademy.html" in your web browser (Chrome, Firefox, or Edge).

================================================================================
                         STEP 2: CHOOSE YOUR PILOT
================================================================================

You'll see 4 awesome avatars. Click on the one that looks most like you:

  🟦 ZANDER  - Blue theme (cool and calm!)
  🟩 ZEKE    - Green theme (nature lover!)
  🟪 OBI     - Purple theme (creative genius!)
  🟧 JEDI    - Orange theme (bold adventurer!)

Then click "Begin Training" - and you're in!

================================================================================
                    STEP 3: YOUR FIRST MISSION
================================================================================

You'll see 9 mission cards. Start with WEEK 1 (it's the top-left one).

Click on it, and you'll see:

  📚 LESSON - Read the cool rocket facts!
  ✅ ACTIVITIES - Click each one as you do it
  ⏱️  TIMER - Counts how long you're spending
  📝 QUIZ - Test your knowledge (need 80% to pass!)
  ✏️  JOURNAL - Write your thoughts

================================================================================
                    IMPORTANT RULES TO REMEMBER
================================================================================

⏱️  SPEND AT LEAST 60 MINUTES
   The timer will warn you if you try to finish too fast!
   Real learning takes time!

✅  COMPLETE ALL ACTIVITIES
   Click each activity when you finish it.
   They turn green when done!

📝  WRITE IN YOUR JOURNAL
   There's no wrong answer! Just share your thoughts.
   It saves automatically!

📋  PASS THE QUIZ
   You need 80% (that's 4 out of 5 correct).
   If you don't pass, you can try again!

================================================================================
                         WEEKLY CHECKLIST
================================================================================

Each week, do this:

□ 1. Read the lesson content
□ 2. Do ALL the hands-on activities
□ 3. Check off each activity as you finish
□ 4. Watch the timer (at least 60 minutes!)
□ 5. Write in your reflection journal
□ 6. Take the quiz (get 80% or higher)
□ 7. Click "Complete Mission"
□ 8. Move to the next week!

================================================================================
                    COACH CISCO'S SAFETY TIPS
================================================================================

Your instructor Coach Cisco always says:

  🛡️  "Safety first, fun second, rockets third!"

This means:
  - Always wear eye protection during launches
  - Never point rockets at people
  - Wait for the "All Clear" before retrieving
  - Ask an adult for help with real rockets!

================================================================================
                         TROUBLE?
================================================================================

If something doesn't work:

❓ "My avatar won't load!"
   → Refresh the page and relaunch the app

❓ "My progress disappeared!"
   → Did you clear your browser history? Don't do that!
   → Make sure you're using the same browser

❓ "The quiz won't let me pass!"
   → You need 80% (4 out of 5 correct)
   → Read the questions carefully!

❓ "I can't complete Week 9!"
   → Did you check all 8 items on the pre-flight checklist?
   → All must be checked before you can launch!
